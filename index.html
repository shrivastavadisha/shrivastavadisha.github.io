Hello everyone! I am Disha Shrivastava, a PhD student in Machine Leaning at Mila, working with Hugo Larochelle and Danny Tarlow. I worked at Google Brain, Montreal as a Student Researcher from Auguest 2019 to June 2022. Currently I am working as a Research Scientist Intern at DeepMind London .

Earlier, I worked at IBM Research, India as a Research Software Engineer for two years. My work focussed on unsupervised construction of knowledge graphs, metrics for computational creativity and topical coherence, and reasoning for maths question-answering. I graduated from IIT Delhi with a Masters in Computer Technology where I developed a data and model-parallel framework for training of deep networks in Apache Spark. I hold a Bachelors degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from BIT Mesra.

My current areas of research include program synthesis, large language models of code and adaptation for program understanding and generation. I am also interested in meta-learning and out-of-distribution generalization. I co-organized the Deep Learning for Code Workshop at ICLR 2022 and AIPLANS Workshop at NeurIPS 2021. I started the ML4Code Reading Group at Mila. My hobbies are singing, reading books and occasional blogging!
